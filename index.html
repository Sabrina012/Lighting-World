Welcome to Lighting World!

This is a website dedicated to helping theater light designers organize and create plans for their ideas.

Here is how to navigate the site:

New show: This tab is meant to be a place to hold information for a each new show that a person is creating.

Saved shows: The saved shows tab is a place to open past show files that you made on lighting world!

Tips & Videos: This tab is meant to help guide new and old light designers when making their specific show by providing information through written and video tips.

Inventory: This is an interactive page where people are able to track the lights and wires they have access to.
